SFI EMEA UNIT


SFI EMEA College unit is the left-wing student orgranisation of emea college of arts and science.

SFI is committed to fighting for the rights of students, including affordable education, better infrastructure, and a democratic and inclusive academic environment. We believe that education is a fundamental right, and that every student deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or financial situation.
